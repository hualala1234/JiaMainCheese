ABOUT

This restaurant offers delicious options that are both affordable and high-quality. Our three pasta dishes come in a variety of flavors and generous portions, perfect for sharing a great time with friends. Don’t miss out on our signature tiramisu dessert, where rich coffee and creamy cheese combine to make a treat that’s also picture-perfect! Our handmade pizza, with its soft crust and abundant toppings, is baked to crispy perfection, giving you a bite full of happiness each time. Whether it’s a casual meetup or a group gathering, this is the ideal spot to enjoy good food and great conversations!

Our Menu


Salad

Our salads are a refreshing blend of vibrant, seasonal ingredients designed to bring flavor, texture, and nourishment to every bite. Starting with crisp, leafy greens as a base, we layer in a variety of colorful vegetables like juicy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and sweet bell peppers for a balanced taste profile. Each salad is thoughtfully crafted with a mix of toppings that may include creamy avocado, tangy cheeses, or toasted nuts and seeds, adding both richness and crunch.

Seafood pasta

Seafood pasta is a beloved dish that combines tender pasta with the fresh flavors of the ocean. Featuring ingredients like shrimp, mussels, clams, or scallops, it’s typically prepared with sauces that highlight the seafood’s natural sweetness—often a light garlic-infused olive oil, a tangy tomato base, or a subtle white wine reduction. Popular in Mediterranean and Italian cuisine, seafood pasta is elevated with touches like fresh herbs, a hint of lemon, or a dash of chili flakes. Perfect as both a refined main course and a comforting classic, seafood pasta offers a satisfying blend of textures and tastes for any occasion.

Risotto

Our Red Sauce Steak Risotto is a hearty, flavorful dish that blends tender beef with creamy risotto, all brought together by a rich, tangy tomato-based sauce. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of textures and flavors, starting with perfectly cooked risotto, which absorbs the vibrant flavors of the sauce and creates a soft, creamy foundation. The red sauce is crafted from ripe tomatoes, slow-simmered with garlic, herbs, and a hint of spice, adding depth and a touch of acidity that elevates every ingredient.

Pistachio Ham Pizza

Our Pistachio Ham Pizza offers a unique twist on classic pizza flavors, blending savory and nutty elements in every bite. This delicious creation features a crisp, hand-tossed crust topped with creamy pistachio sauce, which adds a subtly rich flavor. Layered with thin slices of savory ham and a hint of fresh mozzarella, each slice combines soft, melted cheese with the satisfying crunch of pistachios. The harmony of textures and flavors creates a balanced yet adventurous taste experience. Perfect for those looking to explore a refined and innovative pizza, this dish is both satisfying and surprisingly refreshing.

Dessert

Our cakes are the perfect blend of indulgence and artistry, crafted with the finest ingredients to offer a delightful experience in every bite. Each cake is made from scratch, using premium butter, rich cocoa, and fresh eggs to create a moist, flavorful base. We offer a variety of cakes to satisfy all tastes, from decadent chocolate layers to light, airy vanilla sponges, and even indulgent fruit-filled options.
